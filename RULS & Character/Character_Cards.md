## **Feed the Kraken Character Cards** 

## **1. Captain** 

Reveal this card at the start of the game. You are the first captain. Take the captain’s logbook as proof that you are the captain. Discard this card and draw an additional character card. 

## **2. Kleptomaniac** 

Choose a player. Steal one gun from that player’s supply and add it to yours. 

## **3. Troublemaker** 

Immediately after guns are revealed: Choose a player. Each of that player’s guns count as two guns for the current mutiny. 

## **4. Gunsmith** 

Discard a gun to reveal this card. While this card is revealed, you always get one of your used guns back after a successful mutiny. 

## **5. Peacemaker** 

Immediately after guns are revealed: Choose a player. That player returns their revealed guns to their supply. They do not count towards the current mutiny. 

## **6. Gunslinger** 

Add two guns from the general supply to your personal supply. 

## **7. Minstrel** 

After the captain appoints a navigation team: Choose two players. They do not participate in the upcoming mutiny. 

## **8. Bosun** 

Before navigation cards are drawn: Exchange the lieutenant badge for the navigator badge on the current navigation team. 

## **9. Herbalist** 

Before the captain appoints a navigation team: Move one off-duty sign to another player. That player is off-duty instead. 

## **10. Look-Out** 

Look at the top navigation card of the draw pile. You may discard that card facedown, or leave it on the draw pile. 

## **11. Master Strategist** 

Immediately after guns are revealed: If you do not become the new captain, you will get your revealed guns back after the mutiny. 

## **12. Smuggler** 

Before navigation cards are drawn: Choose a player who has been assigned the captain or lieutenant role. That player draws three navigation cards instead of two. 

## **13. Agitator** 

After the captain appoints a navigation team: Choose two players. They must both reveal at least one gun in the next mutiny. 

## **14. Consultant** 

Before the captain appoints a navigation team: You appoint the next lieutenant. Then the captain appoints the next navigator. 

## **15. Chief Cook** 

After the captain appoints a navigation team: Move the captain role clockwise to the next player with the fewest navigation cards (résumés) in front of them. 

## **16. Rabble-rouser** 

Immediately after guns are revealed: The current mutiny requires half the amount of guns to be successful (rounded up). 

## **17. Archivist** 

Before navigation cards are drawn: Choose a player who has been assigned the captain or lieutenant role. That player may discard their navigation cards to draw two new ones. 

## **18. Mentor** 

Choose a player. Turn the chosen player’s character card facedown. That character may be activated a second time. 

## **19. Spiritualist** 

During a turn in which a yellow navigation card was played: Choose two players. Both players must give one gun to a player of your choice. 

## **20. Debt Collector** 

After the captain appoints a navigation team: Choose a player within the navigation team. That player must give the other players in the navigation team one gun each. 

## **21. Equalizer** 

After the captain appoints a navigation team: The next mutiny requires only one gun to be successful. Each player may only reveal up to one gun. 

## **22. Instigator** 

Immediately after guns are revealed: Choose a player. That player may add all their guns to the current mutiny. If the player does not, then you turn our character card facedown after the mutiny.
